Case study parameter settings
================================

This file reports the parameter values used in the case study of the stochastic perishable production-inventory-routing problem (PPIRP).

Parameter	Value
c_ij	3.5 * distance
F_0	0.165
F^*	0.377
eta	2.63
bar_e	22.6
e^*	0.8
hat_e	0.8
E	0.80
v	45
kappa	0.8
h_it	60
s_it	200
w_it	120
bar_Q	25
r_omega_t	r_omega_t in [bar_r(1 - 0.2), bar_r(1 + 0.2)], where bar_r = 200
I_0	80
I_i	30
b_t	50
m_t	20
z	3

Notes
-----
c_ij: transportation cost associated with arc (i, j), calculated as 3.5 times the corresponding distance.
F_0: fuel consumption per unit distance for an empty vehicle.
F^*: fuel consumption per unit distance for a fully loaded vehicle.
eta: carbon emission conversion factor.
bar_e: unit carbon emission generated by production at the factory.
e^*: unit carbon emission generated by holding products at customer nodes.
hat_e: unit carbon emission generated by holding products at the factory.
E: fuel consumption rate of the refrigeration unit during transportation.
v: average vehicle speed.
kappa: confidence level used in the chance constraints.
h_it: unit inventory holding cost.
s_it: unit stockout cost.
w_it: unit waste cost.
bar_Q: vehicle capacity.
r_omega_t: scenario-dependent production capacity.
I_0: storage capacity of the factory.
I_i: storage capacity of customer node i.
b_t: fixed production setup cost.
m_t: unit production cost.
z: shelf life.
